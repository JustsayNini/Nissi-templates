February 14th: Valentines Day

What's the deal with Valentines Day?

Ah, yes - the month of love. Roses, hearts, and spontaneous love confessions everywhere.

You all should be ashamed of yourself.

For most of you, Valentines isn't really about true love. If it was, you'd be going to church on that day because God is literally the definition of love. But we won't see you at church. We'll see you in that girl's/boy's bed.

Then we'll see a video of you crying over that person leaving you next week Tuesday in your whatsapp status.

But sure, Valentine's Day is a day everyone (who celebrates it) enjoys with a passion. And for that you can thank Saint Valentine(s)!
